Milestones & Deliverables

WHAT – These are Our Deliverables Our leadership team is committed to delivering tangible results.

Inclusivity: All candidates who did not get elected will be offered Committee Chair positions — because we need ALL hands on deck and cannot waste members with ideas and drive.

Values: Bring back Respect, Courtesy, Civility, Brotherhood, Unity and Pride in our Sargodhian spirit and traditions.

Here's what we aim to achieve in 2026:

  • January 15, 2026: First Executive Board Meeting at 1800 hrs
  • Ongoing: Weekly Executive Board Meetings and Monthly Full Board Meetings
  • February 1, 2026: Complete 501(c)(3) Incorporation
  • May 1, 2026: Ratify By-Laws, Constitution, Vision, and Mission Statements
  • October 1, 2026: Launch fully integrated membership portal and complete digital operating platform
  • November 1, 2026: Announce next elections
  • December 17–19, 2026: Conduct elections
  • December 19, 2026: Host Reunion Gala
